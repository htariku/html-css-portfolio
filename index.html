About Me

My Name is Helen Tariku, I am 23 years old and a recent grad from Virginia Commonwealth University. I am currently working at Virginia Hospital Center as a certified Medical Laboratory Technologist in their Microbiology, Hematology, and Chemistry department. I basically analyze and run tests for various specimens and body fluids to see if there are any abnormalities. Alongside that, I also run Covid PCR testing with the newly emerged and ongoing pandemic taking place right now.
During my time as a Medical technologist, I have found a great interest in instrumentation and grew more curious of the roles that our engineers and information technologists play. I have enrolled in George Washington Coding Bootcamp to expand my learning, especially as the world of technology is constantly growing (even in healthcare). With my new found skill I hope to contribute my creativity and ideas to the field and craft my skills overtime.
